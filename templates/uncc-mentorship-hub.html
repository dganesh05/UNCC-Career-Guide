Frequently Asked Questions

How much time commitment is expected for mentorship? +

The time commitment varies depending on the mentorship program and individual arrangements between mentors and mentees. Typically, mentors and mentees meet for 1-2 hours per month, with additional communication as needed. Most mentorship relationships last for 3-6 months, but some continue for longer periods based on mutual agreement.

Can I have more than one mentor? +

Yes, you can have multiple mentors, especially if you're seeking guidance in different areas or industries. We recommend starting with one mentor to establish a strong relationship, then expanding to additional mentors if needed. Our platform allows you to connect with up to three active mentors at once to ensure you can manage these relationships effectively.

What if my mentorship match isn't working out? +

We understand that not all mentorship pairings will be perfect matches. If you feel your mentorship isn't working out, first try discussing your concerns directly with your mentor/mentee. If issues persist, you can contact our Mentorship Coordinator who can provide guidance or help facilitate a new match. There's no penalty for ending a mentorship that isn't meeting your needs.

How are mentors vetted? +

All mentors in our program go through a comprehensive vetting process. This includes verification of their professional background and experience, reference checks, completion of mentor training, and a commitment to our mentorship code of conduct. We also collect ongoing feedback from mentees to ensure mentors are providing valuable guidance and maintaining professional relationships.
